## Roll For Shoes
Roll for Shoes is an adventure game

### Themes:
Each theme guides the adventure in a different way. The descriptions for the themes are below.

**The Heist**:
You are part of a heist team, trying to steal a high-value, treasured item from a security area.
Choose the best actions to successfully avoid security, take the treasure, and escape.

**Outbreak Hotel**:
You are at a popular hotel resort, and must make your way to safety with a ragtag group of survivors 
following the outbreak of a highly contagious zombie-spawning virus. Choose the best actions to guide the
group to success and survival.

**The Stranger**:
You are the citizen of a small frontier town that is visited by an ominous stranger looking to pick a fight.
Choose the best actions to interact with the stranger, and win the fight.

__Instructions and Rules:__
1. Enter your character's name and the theme you would like your adventure to be
2. The game master gives a scenario, and the player responds with their move
3. Both the player and the game master roll a D6
4. If the player's roll is higher than the game master's, their move is a success. Otherwise, it fails
5. The game master will progress the story along depending on the player's outcome
